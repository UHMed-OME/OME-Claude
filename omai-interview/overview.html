OMAI gives medical students unlimited, realistic residency interview practice with an AI interviewer — available any time, with instant feedback.

The Challenge

Residency interviews are high-stakes.
Practice opportunities are not.

  • Residency match outcomes significantly shape a physician's career — yet most students have little structured preparation beyond one or two informal mock interviews.
  • Coaching and practice resources are unevenly distributed, placing students from smaller programs at a disadvantage before they ever walk into a panel.
  • Faculty time is precious and finite. Students need a way to rehearse on their own, at their own pace, as many times as it takes.
